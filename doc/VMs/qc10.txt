"eQC-10" - EPSON QC-10/QX-10 emulator for Win32
								4/3/2008

--- Binaries

	qc10.exe	standard (green monitor)
	qc10cms.exe	with color monitor subboard

--- Internal ROM images

	IPL.ROM		IPL 2KB, 4KB or 8KB
	FONT.ROM	4KB (8x16, 16bytes*256chars)
	MFONT.ROM	MultiFonts ROM card (see also qc10/readme)

	note: qc10csm.exe does not refer font.rom

--- Key maps

	Virtual		PC
	--------------------------------
	BREAK		F11
	PAUSE		F12
	HELP		PAGE UP
	SCRN		PAGE DOWN
	SF1-SF4		SHIFT + F1-F4
	LF		SHIFT + ENTER
	GRAPH		ALT
	CLS		END
