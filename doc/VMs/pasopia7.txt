"EmuPIA" - TOSHIBA PASOPIA emulator for Win32
"EmuPIA7" - TOSHIBA PASOPIA7 emulator for Win32
								1/5/2008

--- Internal ROM images

	For PASOPIA:

	TBASIC.ROM	T-BASIC ROM / PAC SLOT1
	OABASIC.ROM	OA-BASIC ROM
	FONT.ROM	FONT ROM
	KANJI.ROM	KANJI ROM (optional)

	At least, TBASIC.ROM or OABASIC.ROM and FONT.ROM are required.

	For PASOPIA7:

	BIOS.ROM	IPL/BIOS ROM
	BASIC.ROM	BASIC ROM / PAC SLOT1
	FONT.ROM	FONT ROM
	KANJI.ROM	KANJI ROM (optional)

	At least, BIOS.ROM, BASIC.ROM and FONT.ROM are required.

	FONT.ROM in this archive is created from free font images.
	(free rom for FM-7 emulator, and Elisa-Font)
	KANJI.ROM can be created from Windows font (MS Gothic)
	by pasopia7/mkrom.exe in this arichive.

    Optional:
	FDDSEEK.WAV     Sound file of FDD SEEKING.
	RELAY_ON.WAV    Sound file of turning on a CMT's RELAY.	
	RELAYOFF.WAV    Sound file of turning off a CMT's RELAY.

--- About PAC SLOT emulation

	PAC SLOT1:
		Support Language/OS cart

	PAC SLOT2 for PASOPIA:
		#1	RAM PAC2, 32KB

	PAC SLOT2 for PASOPIA7:
		#1	Joy stick adapter (2 sticks)
		#2	KANJI ROM
		#3	RAM PAC2, 32KB
		#4	RAM PAC2, 32KB
