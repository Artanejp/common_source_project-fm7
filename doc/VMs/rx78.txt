"eRX-78" - BANDAI RX-78 Emulator for Win32
								1/5/2008

--- Internal ROM image

	IPL.ROM		$0000-$1FFF

--- Cart ROM images

	Cart ROM is mapped to $2000-$5FFF

    Optional:
	RELAY_ON.WAV    Sound file of turning on a CMT's RELAY.	
	RELAYOFF.WAV    Sound file of turning off a CMT's RELAY.
